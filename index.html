❤️ Our Relationship Reset

Every relationship has arguments. Ours has had more than enough. I don't want us to keep hurting each other over things that can be fixed through understanding.

This isn't about blaming you. It isn't about blaming me either. It's about giving us one chance to understand each other better and create rules that actually work for both of us.

1. What do you think is the biggest cause of our arguments?

2. During our arguments, how do you usually feel?

3. What do you think I could improve?

4. What do you think you could improve?

5. When one of us is angry, what should happen?

6. Which matters more?

7. Which behavior should never happen?

8. How should we solve disagreements?

9. Which quality is most important?

10. Do you believe our relationship is worth fighting for?
